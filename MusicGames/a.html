Phigros

Phigros is a rhythm game, a non-commercial music game created by Pigeon Games, a group of rhythm game enthusiasts that was founded through the bilibili video site. The unique feature of Phigros is that the spectrum is not stereotyped. Non-stereotypical spectrum means that the game spectrum can constantly change, for example, in the game, the decision line is not fixed in the same position, but will move up and down, rotate, jump, and even disappear, which makes the game difficult. Every move of the line in the game is set by the spectrographer, not random

A Dance Of The Fire And Ice

Dance of Ice and Fire is a music game. Players need to follow the rhythm of the music by tapping the keyboard and manipulating the small ball in the narrow road towards the light. The vastness of the stars and the moon, the vastness of the universe, more than 12 different star continents and fantastic music waiting to be experienced. After completing the first six levels, you will unlock Speed Mode and challenge the limits. Players can also create their own cards from the PC and publish them on steam's Creative Workshop.

With just one button, control the hovering double stars, tread the swaying steps, and constantly advance on a winding road that follows the rhythm of the music, and explore the musical universe. The rhythm is cleverly presented in visual form. This will be a challenging musical journey, keep a steady mind and follow your inner rhythm, this is not a game that relies on quick reactions

Arcaea

Arcaea is a three-dimensional music game developed by lowiro and currently available on iOS, Android, and Nintendo Switch. The mobile version of the game was released on March 9, 2017, and the NS version went live on May 18, 2021. The game is known for its unique three-dimensional game mode, with a partner system and some partners having their own personal story, which revolves around light and opposition. The game has a World mode, you can play freely or specify songs to get progress, get tracks, partners and other elements. The game has a potential value system, which can calculate the information of the player's history of playing tracks, and obtain a value to measure their own strength.
